# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis – Project
## project objectives
1.analyze customer purchasing behavior and understand buying patterns
2. identify total customers, purchase amount, and average spending
3.study shopping types (online, store pickup, etc.) and customer preferences
4. analyze category-wise ratings and popular product categories
5.identify factors influencing customer satisfaction and buying decisions
6.create an interactive dashboard for better visualization and insights

#question(KPI)
1.What is the total number of customers?
2.What is the total purchase amount?
3.What is the average purchase value?
4.What is the average purchase frequency per customer?
5.Which customers have the highest spending?
6.How many repeat customers are there?
